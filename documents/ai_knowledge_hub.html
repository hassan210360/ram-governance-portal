09 AI Knowledge Hub

Level 1 – Policy

The AI Knowledge Hub promotes the responsible adoption of artificial intelligence technologies across the organization while ensuring alignment with governance, ethical standards, and operational efficiency.

All AI tools used by the organization must comply with internal governance policies related to data security, transparency, and responsible usage.
