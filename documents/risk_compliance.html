07 Risk & Compliance Framework

Level 1 – Policy

The Risk & Compliance framework establishes the principles for identifying, assessing, monitoring, and mitigating organizational risks while ensuring compliance with legal, regulatory, and internal governance requirements.

All departments must maintain risk registers and periodically review compliance obligations relevant to their operations.

Level 2 – Procedures

  • Risk Identification
  • Risk Assessment & Scoring
  • Risk Mitigation Planning
  • Compliance Monitoring
  • Risk Reporting to Management

Level 3 – Work Instructions

  1. Identify operational and strategic risks
  2. Document risks in the risk register
  3. Assess probability and impact
  4. Define mitigation controls
  5. Monitor risk status quarterly

Level 4 – Forms & Templates

  • Risk Register
  • Compliance Checklist
  • Incident Report Form
