Tenant & Customer Service

17 Tenant & Customer Service Framework

Level 1 - Policy

The Tenant & Customer Service framework ensures high-quality tenant engagement, rapid issue resolution, and continuous improvement in customer satisfaction.

Customer complaints must be acknowledged within 24 hours and resolved within service level agreements defined by management.

Level 2 - Procedures

  • Tenant Onboarding
  • Customer Complaint Management
  • Service Request Handling
  • Customer Satisfaction Monitoring

Level 3 - Work Instructions

  1. Receive tenant inquiry
  2. Register ticket in CRM
  3. Assign service team
  4. Resolve issue
  5. Confirm resolution with tenant

Level 4 - Forms & Templates

  • Customer Complaint Form
  • Service Request Form
  • Customer Satisfaction Survey
